About


In the context of a hackathon, the word "hack" is used to describe how multiple technologies can be used together in a new and innovative way. ! Single Hack is a 12-hour hackathon where we will bring a software to to real life to find our significant other!
! Single Hack is held by KWCSSA,and it is the first for KWCSSA to host a hackthon. We are open to students in any faculty from any schools. We hope and welcome you to join the !Single Hack community.

FAQ

Q: Will there be prizes?

A: Yes, of course! We will will select the three best hacks based on creativity, technical difficulty, design, and usefulness. Prizes for first, second and third place TBA.


Q: What should I bring?

A: Please bring a valid university ID, a computer (preferably a laptop), chargers, and any hardware you will use for your hack.


Q: What size are the teams? What happens if I don’t have a team?

A: The teams are a maximum of 4 people. You can start to hack already with a team or you can meet with people at the event who also don’t have a team, don’t worry!

Q: What is KWCSSA?

A: Founded in 2006, previously known as University of Waterloo Chinese Students and Scholars Association (UWCSSA) is an independent, non-profit organization. As the largest and most influential Chinese organization in the Kitchener-Waterloo area, KWCSSA is dedicated to providing various services and to assisting Chinese students/scholars in the region of Waterloo. We promote intellectual, social, and cultural activities for local Chinese students and scholars.


Q: But I know nothing about health/coding?!

A: Just dont worry! You can come with an open mind, find a team, learn a lot, and most importantly, have fun.


Q: What if I have more questions?

A: We're always open at it@uwcssa.ca. Send us an email, and we'll help you out!
